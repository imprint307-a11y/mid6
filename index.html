About Me

A warm, ethical, and ambitious approach to modern dentistry.

I am a newly graduated dentist with a strong passion for oral health, smile confidence, preventive care, and patient-centered clinical practice. During my university training, I developed a deep appreciation for how dentistry combines science, precision, communication, and compassion.

My professional goal is to grow into a trusted clinician who delivers safe, evidence-based, and beautiful dental care. I am especially interested in restorative dentistry, aesthetic composite work, smile design, dental photography, prevention, and community oral health education.

I am detail-oriented, calm under pressure, highly respectful of patient privacy, and eager to learn from experienced dentists, clinical mentors, workshops, research opportunities, and hands-on training programs.
